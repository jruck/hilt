# Conversational AI Podcast

A podcast where each episode is a conversation between a human host and an AI co-host exploring a topic in depth. Not scripted — the AI brings research and the human brings experience and skepticism.

The format would be:
1. Human picks a topic
2. AI prepares background research
3. They have a real conversation, recorded
4. Light editing for clarity, but keep the natural flow

Could be interesting as a way to demonstrate how human-AI collaboration actually works in practice, not in theory.
