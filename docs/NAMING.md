## What This App Actually Is

**The Core Problem:**
- Deep focused work builds a mental model - 10, 20 sessions, all interconnected
- You're locked in, in the zone, everything makes sense
- Then life happens. You step away.
- You come back: "Now where was I?"

**What the app provides:**
- A **harness** for fast-moving AI work
- **Observability** of what you're actually doing
- A **bridge back into the flow state** you were in
- **Context resurrection** - returning feels like resuming, not starting over

---

## The Slipstream Concept

### What is Slipstreaming/Drafting?
Following closely behind something to reduce energy expenditure and maintain momentum. The leader does the hard work breaking through resistance, and the follower glides in their wake.

### Why It Fits This App
- **Past-you did the work** - built the mental model, established context
- **Present-you drafts behind that work** - slides back into the flow with less effort
- The app is literally a way to **ride in your own slipstream**

## The Clear Candidates (Deep Dive)

### **Previously**
*Like "Previously on..." before a TV episode*

The app is literally a "Previously on your work" experience. It catches you up on what happened before you stepped away.

**Pros:**
- Instantly communicates the purpose
- TV recap energy that everyone understands
- Could style as "Previously..." or "Previously On"
- Self-aware, slightly meta
- Works: "Let me check Previously"

**Cons:**
- Not laugh-out-loud funny
- Common word (but not as an app name)
- Might feel too serious

---

### **Tab Rot**
*When your mental tabs go stale*

Dev humor. Your brain's tabs have rotted. The app fights tab rot. Self-deprecating, instantly relatable to anyone who's had 47 browser tabs open.

**Pros:**
- Pure dev humor
- Self-deprecating in the best way
- Original - nobody is using this
- "How's your work?" "Full of tab rot"
- Describes the problem (ironic naming)

**Cons:**
- "Rot" is negative
- Two words
- Might sound like the disease, not the cure

---

### **Oh Right**
*The exclamation when something clicks*

"Oh right, I was working on that!" The moment of remembering. The name IS the experience.

**Pros:**
- Human, relatable
- Self-aware humor
- The exact moment the app enables
- Simple, easy to say
- Works: "Let me check Oh Right"

**Cons:**
- Two words
- Might be TOO simple
- Not particularly funny (just nice)

---

### **Vestibule**
*The small room between outside and inside*

You're outside your work. You need to transition back in. The vestibule is that architectural transition space.

**Pros:**
- Unusual word, memorable
- Perfect metaphor for transition
- Nobody is using this
- Intellectual without being pretentious

**Cons:**
- Not funny at all
- Sounds clinical
- Requires explanation
