# Quarterly Review

Structured reflection on Q1 2026. What shipped, what didn't, what to focus on next quarter.

## Structure (Draft)

1. **Shipped** — Projects completed, milestones hit
2. **In Progress** — What carried over and why
3. **Dropped** — Things intentionally deprioritized
4. **Surprises** — Unexpected wins or obstacles
5. **Next Quarter** — Top 3 priorities

## Notes

Last quarter's review is in the archive. The format worked well — keep the same structure but add a "lessons learned" section. The surprises section was the most useful part for course-correcting.
