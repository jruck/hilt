# Saturday Briefing — Feb 15, 2026

## Progress

Spent most of the day on two things:

1. **Motion sensor debounce** — The lighting rules were firing multiple times per trigger. Added a configurable debounce window. Testing at 200ms, 500ms, and 1s thresholds. 200ms feels right for hallway sensors, 500ms for rooms.

2. **Companion planting matrix** — The data model is clean now. Each plant has a `companions` and `adversaries` array. The UI renders a color-coded grid. Green = good pairing, red = avoid, gray = neutral.
